Star & Cody's 10 Year Vow Renewal

Hello friends

It has been ten year since we eloped to the beaches of Key West and we have delighted in every second of our marriage since. We have celebrated many victories, together and with every struggle we face our love for one another grows stronger.

Our life together

It was very obvious to us that we were a perfect match after our first date. The feeling of calm and comfort we brought each other was unmatched. We made our first commitment when we adopted our grumpy, but adorable puppy Grimm. We then spent four years traveling and having fun adventures together. Our life became even better and more beautiful when we decided to become parents. Our relationship has developed and become stronger since and we have been able to watch each other grow into amazing people along the way.

Vow renewal

When it comes to our most intimate moments, like the birth of our babies and our wedding day, we usually prefer to keep it between the two of us, but as we approach our ten year anniversary we decide to celebrate with our friends. A vow renewal to us means an opportunity to look foward to the future and what is yet to be and at the same time we get to celebrate our past.
